Why CGP University

At CGP University, we blend academic excellence with purpose-driven living.

With a vibrant campus life, world-class facilities, and a diverse community, you'll innovate, connect, and lead. Our faith-based approach fosters holistic growth, preparing you to be a global thought leader and change-maker.

Located in Ogu, Rivers State, Nigeria, CGP University is a private institution established in 2025. We offer certificates, diplomas, undergraduate, and postgraduate programs fully accredited by the National Universities Commission (NUC).

Admission Requirements
Undergraduate Admission

📋 Minimum Entry Requirements

  • West African Senior School Certificate (WAEC/NECO) with minimum of 5 credits including English Language and Mathematics
  • Credits must be obtained in not more than two sittings
  • JAMB UTME score of minimum 180 (subject to review by programme)
  • Direct Entry: Minimum of 2 A-Level passes or OND/NCE with upper credit
  • Relevant subject combinations as specified per faculty/department

📁 Required Documents

  • Completed online application form
  • WAEC/NECO/NABTEB result (original or scanned copy)
  • JAMB result slip and admission letter
  • Birth certificate or statutory declaration of age
  • Recent passport photographs (4 copies)
  • Letter of recommendation from school principal or employer
  • Medical fitness certificate from a registered physician

🔢 How to Apply — Step by Step

1
Create an AccountVisit the CGP University admissions portal and create your applicant profile.
2
Choose Your ProgrammeBrowse available programmes and select the one that matches your goals and qualifications.
3
Upload DocumentsScan and upload all required documents as listed above in PDF or JPEG format.
4
Pay Application FeeComplete your application fee payment via the secure online payment gateway.
5
Await ConfirmationYou will receive an email confirmation within 5–10 working days. Track your status online.
6
Acceptance & EnrollmentUpon offer of admission, accept your place, pay acceptance fee, and complete enrollment online.

Admission Requirements
Postgraduate Admission

🎓 Entry Requirements

  • A good Bachelor's degree (minimum Second Class Lower) from a recognised university
  • Postgraduate Diploma (PGD) holders with merit may qualify for some master's programmes
  • Doctoral applicants must hold a relevant master's degree with a minimum of Merit
  • Professional certifications may be considered for some programmes at the faculty's discretion

📁 Required Documents

  • Completed online postgraduate application form
  • Official undergraduate transcripts (sealed by issuing institution)
  • Bachelor's degree certificate (certified copy)
  • Two academic/professional letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose (500–800 words)
  • Current curriculum vitae / résumé
  • Evidence of relevant work experience (where applicable)
  • Research proposal (for doctoral applicants — 1,500–3,000 words)

🔢 How to Apply — Step by Step

1
Register OnlineCreate your postgraduate applicant profile on the CGP University admissions portal.
2
Select Programme & SupervisorFor doctoral programmes, identify a potential supervisor from our faculty directory before applying.
3
Submit DocumentsUpload all required academic and supporting documents securely to the portal.
4
Interview (If Required)Some programmes require a brief interview with the admissions panel — conducted online or in-person.
5
Receive Offer LetterSuccessful applicants receive a conditional or unconditional offer letter within 2–4 weeks.
6
Enrol & RegisterAccept your offer, pay the acceptance fee, and register for your programme modules.
International Applicants
International Student Admission

🌍 Welcome to CGP University

We warmly welcome students from across Africa and beyond. CGP University is committed to building a diverse, global learning community in the heart of Rivers State, Nigeria.

📋 International Entry Requirements

  • Equivalent of Nigerian WAEC (e.g., Cambridge O-Levels, IGCSE) with 5 credits including English and Mathematics
  • Undergraduate degree equivalent for postgraduate applicants
  • English language proficiency: IELTS 6.0+ or TOEFL 80+ (for non-English medium graduates)
  • Valid international passport (minimum 18 months validity)

📁 Additional Documents Required

  • Certified copies of all academic certificates (translated to English if necessary)
  • Letter of sponsorship or proof of financial capability
  • Medical/health certificate and immunisation records
  • Nigerian visa/student permit application support provided upon admission

🤝 Support for International Students

CGP University provides a dedicated International Students Office to assist with visa applications, campus orientation, accommodation, and cultural integration into Nigerian student life.

Financial Support
Scholarships & Financial Aid

💰 Available Awards

  • Founders' Excellence Scholarship — Full tuition waiver for top-performing JAMB entrants (score 260+)
  • Academic Merit Award — 50% tuition reduction for students with minimum 5 distinctions at WAEC
  • Need-Based Bursary — Financial support for students demonstrating genuine economic hardship
  • Covenant Faith Award — For students with outstanding community service and faith-based leadership
  • Research Grant — For postgraduate students engaged in approved research projects

📋 How to Apply for Scholarships

1
Complete Your Admission Application FirstAll scholarship applicants must first have an active admission application or offer.
2
Submit Scholarship FormComplete the scholarship application form available on the financial aid portal.
3
Provide Supporting EvidenceAcademic transcripts, letters of recommendation, and for bursaries, a financial statement.
4
Await DecisionThe scholarships committee reviews all applications. Decisions communicated within 6 weeks.
